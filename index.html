Approach

How we think about operations

We believe in doing things manually first. Before you automate, before you scale, you need to understand what actually works. The best systems come from deeply understanding the work.

We also believe in simplicity. Not every problem needs a tool. Not every process needs documentation. We help you figure out what matters and ignore the rest.
